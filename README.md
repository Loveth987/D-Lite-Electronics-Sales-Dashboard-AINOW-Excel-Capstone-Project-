## Key Insights

Revenue is concentrated in a few top-performing products and categories.

Monthly revenue shows fluctuations, indicating seasonal or inconsistent demand.

Some regions significantly outperform others in revenue generation.

A small number of customers contribute the majority of total revenue.

Several products and categories underperform and require strategic review.




## Recommendations

Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products and categories.

Improve sales performance in low-performing regions through targeted campaigns.

Introduce promotions during low-revenue months to stabilize sales trends.

Implement customer loyalty strategies to retain high-value customers.

Review underperforming products for repricing or discontinuation.
